Our Program

1. Introduces students in underprivileged communities to fundamental computer science concepts.

2. Teaches computer science concepts through interactive methods, unlike the traditional classroom teaching style.

3. Provides mentorship to students from engineers and leader in top companies as well as computer science students in top programs.

4. Creates a strong community of scholars dedicated to becoming knowledgable computer scientists.





Knowledge

You will have access to resources to continually develop your computer science education.

Mentorship

Community

You will meet people from similar backgrounds at all stages of the learning process, from beginners to experienced engineers.
